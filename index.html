DA&O Tool — Design Analysis & Optimization Suite
Developer  ·  MATLAB-Based Computational Framework
Feb 2026 – Present

A modular MATLAB computational suite for streamlining parametric engineering workflows, transforming raw simulation data into optimized designs through a seamless, extensible pipeline architecture. Born from the lesson of over 100 manual iterations at Groupe Atlantic, DA&O codifies the methodological shift from simulation-based to computation-driven design.

  • DoE Module Latin hypercube sampling, full factorial, and Sobol sequences for efficient exploration of high-dimensional design spaces.
  • Sensitivity Analysis Sobol global sensitivity indices to identify dominant design variables and reduce optimization dimensionality.
  • Surrogate Modelling Gaussian Process Regression and polynomial response surfaces for fast, validated approximation of computationally expensive simulation responses.
  • Optimization Multi-parameter gradient-based and evolutionary optimization for design space exploitation.
  • Post-Processing Automated data analysis, visualization, and report generation with real-time workflow progress monitoring.
